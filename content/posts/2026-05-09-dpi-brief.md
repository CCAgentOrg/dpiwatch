# DPI Brief — May 9, 2026

## Today's DPI Updates (7 Layers)

### L1: Identity & Authentication
- **UIDAI Biometric Update Reminder**: UIDAI urged parents to update children's Aadhaar biometrics at ages 5 and 15 to prevent authentication problems affecting school admissions. Regular biometric updates ensure seamless digital identity verification for education and government services[^1].

- **Aadhaar Authentication in Deepfake Scam**: Gujarat Police busted an AI deepfake loan scam targeting social media users, arresting 7 individuals. The fraudsters used manipulated photos to obtain loans, with Aadhaar authentication used during the process. This incident highlights the need for stronger safeguards against AI-generated identity fraud[^2].

### L2: Payments & Financial Rails
- **PNB Launches RuPay Credit Card on UPI**: Punjab National Bank partnered with Kiwi to launch the PNB Kiwi Credit Card on the RuPay network, targeting 180 million customers. The card integrates traditional credit services with digital payments, expanding financial inclusion for semi-urban and rural populations[^3].

- **UPI Transaction Milestones**: India reached a historic milestone with 22.64 billion UPI transactions in March 2026, totaling ₹29.53 lakh crore — the highest single-month volume in history[^4]. UPI now serves 691 banks (up from just 216 in 2016) and has become the world's largest retail fast-payment system by volume[^5].

### L3: Documents & Data Exchange
- **API Setu Status Update**: API Setu reported resolution of "Digilocker Aadhar Fetch Document Errors" on May 2, 2026, restoring smooth document retrieval services for users[^6].

### L4: Commerce & Logistics
- **Limited Updates**: No major ONDC or GeM announcements from the last 24 hours.

### L5: Sectoral Infrastructure
- **eCourts Digital Services**: High Courts across India continue expanding digital court infrastructure. Meghalaya High Court notice board was last updated on May 8, 2026, maintaining real-time access to case information and judicial schedules[^7].

### L6: Governance & Grievance
- **National Awards for e-Governance 2026**: Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances (DARPG) notified the National Awards for e-Governance 2026, featuring 16 awards across seven categories with incentive prizes of ₹10 lakh for Gold awards and ₹5 lakh for Silver awards[^8].

- **CPGRAMS Performance**: The Centralised Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System continues to show strong grievance redressal metrics, with monthly reports demonstrating sustained digital governance improvements across Central Ministries and States/UTs[^9].

### L7: Security, Privacy & Trust
- **DPDP Implementation**: The Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, notified in November 2025, continued implementation in 2026. The framework aims to regulate processing of personal data while balancing innovation with privacy protections[^10].

- **CERT-In Focus on AI Threats**: The CERT-In cyber security agency increased focus on AI-powered threats, including deepfake scams and synthetic identity fraud using Aadhaar authentication[^11].
