# Digital India DPI Brief — March 8, 2026

**Stack Layers Active Today:** L1 Identity, L3 Data Exchange, L4 Commerce, L6 Governance, L7 Regulatory

---

## LAYER 1: Identity & Authentication

### Paperless Aadhaar Enrollment Available
Paperless Aadhaar enrollment process now available via `myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in`. Enhanced face authentication options being rolled out.

**Source:** UIDAI Portal | `https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in`

### NEET 2026 Registration Requires Aadhaar eKYC
NEET (UG) 2026 registration opens today with **mandatory Aadhaar-based eKYC** for candidate verification. Systems using DigiLocker integration.

**Source:** NEET Official | UGC/NTA Circulars

### UP Scholarship Portal: Aadhaar + OTR Mandatory
Samaj Kalyan Vibhag UP requires **Aadhaar-linked One Time Registration (OTR)** for scholarship applications. Students without Aadhaar face eligibility issues.

**Source:** UP Government Circulars | `scholarship.up.gov.in`

### CKYC Updates via DigiLocker
Central KYC (CKYC) records now updatable through **DigiLocker integration**. Banks and financial institutions can fetch updated customer data via API.

**Source:** Central Registry of Securing India | Banking Circulars

## LAYER 6: Governance & Public Service Delivery

### e-Nagar 2.0 Portal Launched
Digital governance portal **e-Nagar 2.0** launched at `nagarapp.in` with **Digilocker, UMANG, and API Setu** integrations. Provides citizen services for urban local bodies.

**Source:** Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs | `nagarapp.in`

### Sarathi Didi Scheme Launched
Uber-style service **"Sarathi Didi"** launched in **UP, Delhi, and 3 other states** — women-driven ride-sharing with government backing. Uses identity verification via Aadhaar.

**Source:** MoHUA | State Government Press Releases
